What is TuffyPub?

TuffyPub is a university oriented live chatroom. TuffyPub aims to integrate with student univerity portals and provide students with a ready to use account and platform to be able get in touch with fellow students. By having a dedicated provided platform students can have acess to communication outside of the classroom without the need to exchange personal information or the need to create a social media acount.

Who is TuffyPub for?

TuffyPub is for students looking to connect with fellow students outside of class whether it be for academic or extracurricular purposes.

How Does TuffyPub Work?

Once logged in users can access various chat rooms according to interest. Once in the chatroom they are free to converse with other users.
